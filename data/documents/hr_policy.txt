EnergyCo HR Policy Document

Onboarding:
New employees must complete onboarding within 30 days of their start date.
Onboarding includes safety training, IT setup, and a meeting with their manager.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid leave per year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal.
Sick leave is separate and requires a medical certificate after 3 consecutive days.

Remote Work:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work requires a stable internet connection and a dedicated workspace.

Performance Reviews:
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December.
Employees are rated on a scale of 1 to 5 across technical skills, collaboration, and delivery.
